Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is MyJapaneseQuest?
A: MyJapaneseQuest is an online subscription-based Japanese tutoring service offering structured learning materials, feedback, and live lessons to help students learn Japanese effectively.
Q: Who is the founder of MyJapaneseQuest?
A: MyJapaneseQuest was founded by Mizuki, an experienced Japanese tutor with over seven years of tutoring experience in the UAE.
Q: What subscription plans do you offer?
A: We offer three subscription plans:
- Basic: AED 100 per month includes grammar lessons, activity sheets, cultural insights, and optional audio/video files.
- Standard: AED 200 per month includes everything in the Basic plan plus feedback and marking of submitted work.
- Premium: AED 500 per month includes everything in the Standard plan plus 2 live face-to-face online classes per month and additional accountability and encouragement.
Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription plan?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Just contact us to make the necessary changes.
Q: What types of learning materials do you provide?
A: Our learning materials include grammar-focused lessons, activity sheets, Japanese cultural insights, and sometimes audio or video files to enhance your learning experience.
Q: How often are live classes conducted?
A: Live classes are conducted twice a month for Premium subscribers. However, you can add more live classes based on student request.
Q: Can I get personalized feedback on my work?
A: Yes, both Standard and Premium subscribers receive personalized feedback on their submitted work.
Q: How do I access the learning materials?
A: Learning materials are accessible through WhatsApp or email, which also allows you to download them for offline use.
Q: What if I miss a live class?
A: If you miss a live class, you can contact us to reschedule or access a recorded version if available.
Q: How do I communicate with my tutor?
A: You can communicate with your tutor via email or WhatsApp for any questions or support you need.
Q: Do you offer any free resources?
A: Yes, we offer a free downloadable PDF on our website that provides tips on how to start learning Japanese. This is a great resource for beginners.
Q: How can I sign up for MyJapaneseQuest?
A: You can sign up through our website. Simply contact us via email and WhatsApp and have a completely free 30-minute consultation.
Q: Do you offer any trial periods?
A: Currently, we do not offer trial periods, but you can start with our Basic plan to get a feel for our materials and services before committing to a higher plan.
Q: How can I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us through the app or email. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
Q: How can I contact MyJapaneseQuest for more information?
A: You can contact us through our website, email us at japanesetutorinabudhabi@gmail.com, or reach out via WhatsApp at +971 502732789.
